# > Desc, Greater

## Desc

Returns positions of elements of it's argument such as
they will be placed in descending sorted order

**Syntax:** ```>x; >[x]```

```o
o)>1 2 3 1 2 9 8 5
5 6 7 2 1 4 0 3
```

**Syntax:** ```x>y; >[x;y]```
